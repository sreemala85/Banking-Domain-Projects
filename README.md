# Banking-Domain-Projects
Data Extraction, Manipulation, Modelling, Warehousing for CASA, Credit Card, Mortgage, PBG portfolios

📊 **Key Dashboards Automated & Delivered:**

**1. CASA & Credit Cards**
✔️Total customers (CASA)
✔️Active vs inactive customers
✔️Existing-to-bank vs new-to-bank active customers
✔️Reasons for customer inactivity
✔️MoM trends since Dec’24 for active & inactive customers
✔️Customer movement (Active ↔ Inactive)
✔️New acquisitions (New-to-Bank vs New-to-Product)
✔️Attrition analysis: MoM trend, active & inactive attrition, top attrition reasons, attrition by nationality

**2. Credit Card Sales Performance**
✔️Total card sales by source (Bank vs Agencies)
✔️Activation performance by agencies
✔️Activated & used card performance
✔️Top 10 card products by activation and usage

**3. Personal Banking Group (PBG)**
✔️Total, active & inactive customers with MoM trends
✔️YoY active customer growth
✔️Products per customer & total products held
✔️Active customer attrition (MoM & YoY)
✔️Customer growth vs target
✔️Product penetration across PBG customers

**4. Mortgage Customers**
✔️Total mortgage customers & loans disbursed (monthly/yearly)
✔️Customer distribution by loan type, customer type, segment & employment status
✔️Loan agreements by loan type and performance status, Avg loan amount, outstanding balance, tenure & tenure left, Loan trends & tenure cohorts
✔️Product penetration for mortgage customers
